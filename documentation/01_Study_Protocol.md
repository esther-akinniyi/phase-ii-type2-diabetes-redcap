# Study Protocol

## Study Title

Phase II Type 2 Diabetes Treatment Study

---

## Protocol Number

T2D-PhaseII-001

---

## Sponsor

Simulated Clinical Research Study

---

## Study Phase

Phase II

---

## Study Design

Prospective, multicenter, observational clinical trial using REDCap Electronic Data Capture (EDC).

---

## Study Objective

To evaluate the safety and effectiveness of an investigational treatment for adults diagnosed with Type 2 Diabetes Mellitus while demonstrating the design and implementation of a REDCap clinical database.

---

## Primary Endpoint

- Change in HbA1c from baseline to follow-up.

---

## Secondary Endpoints

- Change in fasting glucose
- Incidence of adverse events
- Medication adherence
- Clinical improvement

---

## Study Population

Adults diagnosed with Type 2 Diabetes Mellitus.

---

## Inclusion Criteria

- Age ≥18 years
- Confirmed diagnosis of Type 2 Diabetes
- Informed consent obtained

---

## Exclusion Criteria

- Pregnancy
- Type 1 Diabetes
- Participation in another investigational study

---

## Study Visits

1. Screening & Enrollment
2. Medical History
3. Concomitant Medications
4. Adverse Events
5. Follow-up Visit
6. End of Study

---

## Data Collection

All participant data were collected electronically using REDCap Electronic Case Report Forms (eCRFs).

---

## Regulatory Note

This protocol represents a simulated clinical study developed for portfolio and educational purposes and does not involve real participants.
